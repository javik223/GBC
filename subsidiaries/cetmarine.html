Upstream

CETMarine Limited

(Offshore Transportation and Logistics Division)

CETMarine Limited is a subsidiary of CETEnergy, which was created to focus on upstream/platform E&P support services, within the Nigerian oil and gas sector.

When fully operational, our services will include:

  • Vessel Transportation Services to and fro offshore platforms
  • Loading & discharge cargo operations
  • Ocean Towing;
  • Emergency Response
