Gbenga Biobaku & Co. (GBC) is a leading Nigerian commercial law firm located in Lagos, the commercial capital of Nigeria, with a representative office in Port Harcourt, the oil and gas nerve centre of the country. Our principal areas of practice are Oil and Gas, Energy, Maritime, Projects, Foreign Investments, Banking, Capital Markets and general Corporate/ Commercial Law.

We provide legal advisory and related services to a diverse portfolio of clients ranging from multinational corporations to local corporate entities, entrepreneurs, government parastatals and non-governmental organisations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide top quality legal services and solutions which are strategically focused and practically beneficial to our wide and diverse range of clients.
