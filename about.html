Gbenga Biobaku & Co. (GBC) is a leading law firm in Nigeria with primary practice in the business scene.

For over 20years, our Firm has demonstrated its expertise in handling challenging assignments for the most interesting clients. We have achieved extraordinary results with a team of intensely focused members with a focus on solving our clients' most important problems.

Building strong client relationships is the compass of our business strategy. With diverse experience and exceptional professionals, we have continually delivered quality to our clients through innovative legal solutions.

Our clients range from multinationals, oil & energy firms, financial institutions and emerging companies. We also advise governments and public bodies.
